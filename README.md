## 📊 Executive Summary

**Transforming Restaurant Data into Strategic Business Intelligence**

This platform analyzes **1,340+ restaurants across Bangalore**, transforming raw restaurant data into **actionable business insights**. It helps restaurant owners, investors, and food enthusiasts understand market trends, pricing strategies, and customer preferences.

Using **advanced analytics, statistical models, and machine learning**, the system uncovers hidden patterns in the restaurant ecosystem.

### 🎯 Key Business Value

* **97.5% Accurate Success Prediction** using Machine Learning
* **Strategic Market Intelligence** for competitive advantage
* **Personalized Recommendation Engine** for enhanced customer experience
* **Visual Analytics Dashboard** for executive-level insights

## 📈 Dataset Overview

This project uses a **comprehensive restaurant dataset** covering the Bangalore metropolitan food ecosystem.

### Dataset Highlights

* **Total Records:** 1,340 merged restaurant profiles
* **Geographic Coverage:** Bangalore metropolitan area
* **Cuisine Diversity:** 50+ cuisine types
* **Price Range:** ₹100 – ₹2,000+ for two people
* **Rating Scale:** 1.0 – 5.0 customer satisfaction scores
* **Digital Features:** Online delivery & table booking availability

### Data Quality Metrics

* **99.2% complete records** after cleaning
* **No duplicate entries**
* **Standardized cuisine and location formatting**
* **Validated pricing and rating values**
